Affordable Online Nursing Degree Programs in 2025

In 2025, pursuing a nursing degree online has become more accessible and affordable than ever before. The world of healthcare is rapidly evolving, and the demand for qualified nurses continues to grow. But here’s the good news – you don’t have to spend years attending a traditional in-person program to get your nursing degree. With the rise of online education, aspiring nurses can now earn their degrees from the comfort of their own home while maintaining their current jobs or family responsibilities.

If you’re considering becoming a nurse but worried about the cost of education or the flexibility of traditional programs, this is your guide to affordable online nursing degree programs that can help you take the first step toward a fulfilling healthcare career. Whether you’re looking to pursue an associate’s , bachelor’s , or master’s in nursing, we’ve got you covered with options that are not only cost-effective but also highly respected in the healthcare industry.

Why Choose an Online Nursing Degree?

Before we dive into the specific programs, let’s take a moment to consider why pursuing an online nursing degree is such an attractive option in 2025.

  1. Flexibility – Online nursing programs offer the flexibility to study at your own pace. This is especially beneficial for those juggling work, family, or other responsibilities. You can learn when it’s most convenient for you, whether that’s during the evenings, weekends, or even during your lunch breaks.
  2. Cost Savings – Traditional in-person nursing programs can come with high tuition fees, additional costs for commuting, and sometimes even room and board. By studying online, you can cut out those additional expenses and save money. Moreover, many online programs offer financial aid, scholarships, and even discounted tuition for in-state students, making it easier to manage the costs.
  3. Accreditation – Many online nursing programs are accredited by the same organizations that approve traditional programs. This ensures that your degree holds the same value and is recognized by employers across the nation. Accreditation is essential to ensure that the program meets the industry standards and prepares you for the challenges of the nursing profession.
  4. A Growing Field – The nursing field is expected to continue growing. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics , the demand for registered nurses is projected to grow by 9% from 2020 to 2030, much faster than the average for all occupations. By enrolling in an online program now, you’ll be positioned to take advantage of this growing demand.

Top Affordable Online Nursing Degree Programs in 2025

Now that we’ve covered why an online nursing degree can be a great option, let’s explore some of the most affordable programs available in 2025. These programs are not only budget-friendly but also offer strong curriculums, flexible learning options, and solid reputations in the healthcare industry.

1. University of Phoenix – Online RN to BSN Program

The University of Phoenix offers an affordable and flexible RN to BSN (Registered Nurse to Bachelor of Science in Nursing) program. This program is designed for licensed RNs who already have an associate degree or diploma in nursing and want to continue their education to earn a bachelor’s degree.

  • Cost: Around $350 per credit hour, with a total of 120 credits required for graduation.
  • Duration: Typically 12 to 24 months, depending on the student’s schedule and transfer credits.
  • Accreditation: Commission on Collegiate Nursing Education (CCNE) accredited.

The University of Phoenix’s RN to BSN program is recognized for its flexibility and affordable pricing. You can take classes online, and the program offers courses in subjects like leadership, research, and community health, which are crucial for advancing in nursing.

2. Southern New Hampshire University (SNHU) – Online Nursing Programs

Southern New Hampshire University is well-known for offering affordable and high-quality online nursing degrees. They provide several nursing programs, including RN to BSN , Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN) , and even Master’s in Nursing (MSN).

  • Cost: Around $320 per credit hour for the RN to BSN program.
  • Duration: The RN to BSN program can be completed in as little as 14 months.
  • Accreditation: CCNE accredited.

SNHU has a strong reputation for student support and online learning, making it a top choice for those pursuing nursing degrees online. They also offer a wide variety of scholarships and financial aid opportunities to help reduce the overall cost of the program.

3. The College Network – Online RN to BSN Program

The College Network partners with accredited universities to offer RN to BSN programs that are affordable and designed for working professionals. Students can choose from a variety of schools, including Indiana University and University of Louisville , to complete their degree online.

  • Cost: Varies by partnering school, but generally costs less than $350 per credit hour.
  • Duration: Typically 18 to 24 months.
  • Accreditation: Programs offered are accredited by CCNE and other regional accrediting agencies.

The College Network offers a unique opportunity for nurses to complete their degrees through a flexible, self-paced model. They also offer resources to help students prepare for exams and complete their coursework effectively.

4. Walden University – Online Nursing Programs

Walden University offers several online nursing degree programs, including BSN , MSN , and DNP (Doctor of Nursing Practice) options. Their programs are designed for registered nurses who want to advance their education and career without sacrificing their job or personal commitments.

  • Cost: Approximately $400 per credit hour for the BSN program.
  • Duration: The BSN program can be completed in about 12 months.
  • Accreditation: CCNE accredited.

Walden’s online programs are known for their high-quality curriculum and flexible learning options. Their courses are specifically tailored for nurses who want to specialize in areas such as healthcare leadership, public health, or nursing education.

5. University of Southern Mississippi – Online BSN Program

For those interested in pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Nursing , the University of Southern Mississippi offers an affordable online BSN program. This program is designed for those who want to earn their degree while continuing to work in the field.

  • Cost: Around $350 per credit hour.
  • Duration: The program typically takes about 4 years to complete, depending on your schedule.
  • Accreditation: CCNE accredited.

This program provides students with a solid foundation in nursing theory and clinical practice. Students can complete coursework online while completing clinical requirements in their local community.

How to Choose the Best Online Nursing Program for You

Choosing the best online nursing degree program can be a challenge with so many options available. Here are some key factors to consider when making your decision:

  1. Accreditation: Always ensure that the program is accredited by a recognized agency like the CCNE or ACEN (Accreditation Commission for Education in Nursing). This ensures that your degree will be recognized by employers and that the program meets industry standards.
  2. Cost and Financial Aid: Compare the tuition fees for different programs and consider how they fit into your budget. Be sure to research available scholarships, grants, and loan options.
  3. Flexibility: Look for programs that allow you to study at your own pace and fit coursework around your schedule. Online nursing programs should offer asynchronous learning, so you can complete assignments and exams on your time.
  4. Reputation and Support: Choose a program with a strong reputation in the healthcare industry and excellent student support services. This includes advising, tutoring, and career services.
  5. Clinical Requirements: While the coursework can be completed online, most programs require you to complete clinical hours in a healthcare setting. Ensure that the program provides opportunities for clinical placements in your local area.

The Future of Online Nursing Education

As we move further into 2025 and beyond, online nursing programs will continue to evolve and improve. With advances in technology, the quality of online education is better than ever, allowing students to access cutting-edge learning materials and interact with peers and professors from across the globe.

Moreover, with the growing need for healthcare professionals, particularly nurses, the demand for qualified online nursing programs will only increase. In the coming years, it’s likely that even more affordable and accessible programs will become available, allowing even more individuals to pursue careers in nursing without financial or geographical barriers.

If you’re ready to start your nursing journey, 2025 is the perfect time to explore affordable online nursing programs that fit your needs. With the right program, dedication, and support, you can transform your life and begin a rewarding career in the healthcare field.
